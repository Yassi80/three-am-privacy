Privacy Policy — Three A.M.

Effective date: 06.07.2026  ·  Contact: yassi801@gmail.com

Three A.M. is a tool for the moments at night when your mind won't slow down. What you write here is yours. This policy explains, plainly, what the app collects, what it doesn't, and the control you have over it.

Three A.M. is a self-help tool. It is not therapy, medical advice, or an emergency service. If you are in crisis, use the in-app crisis resources or contact your local emergency services.

Who we are

Three A.M. ("the app", "we") is operated by Iassen Gueorguiev. You can reach us at yassi801@gmail.com.

What we collect

We keep this deliberately small.

  • What you write. The words you type when you describe what's looping, and the app's response, are saved so you can revisit them in the morning. This is the most sensitive thing the app handles, and we treat it that way (see How we protect it below).
  • Your email — only if you choose to. The app works anonymously by default. You only have an email on file if you opt to "save your data" by adding one, which links your sessions to an account so they survive reinstalling the app.
  • An anonymous account identifier. When you first open the app it creates an anonymous account so your data can be stored and retrieved. This is not tied to your name or identity unless you add an email.
  • Minimal technical diagnostics. We record small, non-content metrics about the AI requests (such as response time and token counts) to keep the service working. These contain none of what you wrote.

What we do NOT collect

  • No advertising identifiers, and no ads.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No location, contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera data.
  • No device push tokens (reminders are scheduled locally on your device).
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not use your writing to train AI.

How we use what we collect

  • To generate a calm, in-the-moment response to what you wrote.
  • To save your entry so a clearer, daytime version of you can revisit it.
  • To send you a local morning reminder, if you turn that on.
  • To operate, secure, and debug the service.

How we protect it

  • In transit: all data is sent over encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • At rest: the content you write is additionally encrypted by us using AES-GCM-256 before it is stored, with a key that is never placed on your device or in the database alongside the data.
  • Access to your data is restricted, per-account, by database security rules so that one user cannot read another user's content.

Who processes your data (service providers)

We use a small number of providers that process data on our behalf, not for their own purposes:

  • Supabase — hosts the database, authentication, and server functions where your account and encrypted content live.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — the text you write is sent to Anthropic to generate the app's response. Anthropic processes it on our behalf to return a reply and, under their commercial API terms, does not use it to train their models.

We do not share your data with third parties for advertising or their own use.

How long we keep it

You choose. In Settings, "Keep new sessions for" lets you pick 7, 30, 90, or 365 days, or Forever. The default is 90 days. Entries older than your chosen window are removed automatically.

Your choices and rights

  • Delete everything, anytime. In Settings, "Delete my account and data" permanently removes your sessions, morning revisits, and account. This cannot be undone.
  • Stay anonymous. You never have to add an email.
  • Reach us. For any privacy request, contact yassi801@gmail.com.

Children

Three A.M. is intended for adults and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the effective date above and, for material changes, surface a notice in the app.
