Delete your account and data

Three A.M. — operated by Iassen Gueorguiev · Contact: yassi801@gmail.com

You can permanently delete your Three A.M. account and everything associated with it at any time. Deletion removes your account, every session you wrote, every morning revisit, and your profile settings. This cannot be undone.

Delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open Three A.M.
  2. Tap the settings gear (top left of the home screen).
  3. Under Account, tap “Delete my account and data”.
  4. Confirm with “Yes, delete it all”.

Deletion takes effect immediately: your written sessions, morning revisits, profile, and the account itself are permanently removed from our servers.

If you've already uninstalled the app

  • If you added an email to your account: send a deletion request to yassi801@gmail.com from that email address, and we'll delete the account and all associated data.
  • If you used the app anonymously (never added an email): reinstall the app on the same device to reconnect to your anonymous account, then delete from Settings as above. Anonymous accounts aren't linked to your name or email, so we have no way to identify your data from a written request — that's by design.

What is deleted, and when

  • Deleting your account removes all of it: account, written sessions, morning revisits, and profile/settings. We don't keep copies for our own purposes.
  • Independently of deletion, sessions expire automatically based on your retention setting (“Keep new sessions for” — 7/30/90/365 days, or Forever; default 90 days).

Questions about deletion or your data: yassi801@gmail.com. See also our privacy policy.
